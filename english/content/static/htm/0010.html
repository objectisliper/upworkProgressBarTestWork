INTRODUCTION

The security awareness course is intended to teach participants how to identify the many forms of cyber security risks and its potential impacts. We will cover the following topics in this course:

  • INTRODUCTION
  • LESSON 1 – SOCIAL ENGINEERING ATTACKS
  • LESSON 2 – PHYSICAL SECURITY
  • LESSON 3 – EMAIL SECURITY
  • LESSON 4 – SECURE INTERNET USAGE
  • LESSON 5 – SECURE SOCIAL MEDIA USAGE
  • LESSON 6 – USING PUBLIC WI-FI’S
  • LESSON 7 – SECURITY INCIDENTS

This course should take approximately 60 minutes to complete.
